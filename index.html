Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Wallet balance and transaction history
  • App preferences and settings
  • Device tokens and notification preferences

  • Anonymized analytics data (cannot be linked back to you)

After 30 days, all retained data will be permanently deleted from our systems.
